About Us

Our service to you includes the utmost care and attention to your requirements — whether it be a chocolate fountain/fondue for rent or to purchase. From home to commercial use, we strive to help you decide which application would best suite your needs. Our chocolate gifts customized just for you are definitely a special product. They include a wide range of personalized graphic services and the setting up of your designs for memorable events.

Our Services and Service Area

Rentals — Falling for Chocolate’s fountains/fondues are available for rent throughout the Fraser Valley, Vancouver and the lower mainland. Our pricing includes all delivery and pick up charges, unless otherwise noted. A phone call, email or completing our online form ensures a personal consult to meet your needs.

Sales — Our fountain sales are Canada-wide and feature the superb “Sephra” fountains which are available in all sizes for both home and commercial use. The Sephra fountains are an ideal option for both home use and commercial venues.

Our chocolate is of the finest quality made by Callebaut and Belcolade in Belgium and is available in milk, dark and white flavours. And how about having pink or a special colour to go with your event theme!? We also supply other products that compliment the fountains such as fresh fruit, dessert items, a wide dipping assortment and skewers.

It’s the Special Event...

We keep our fountains well maintained and guarantee they are in excellent working condition. Our attendants come attired in professional logoed attire so you know who we are!

How About Some Help?

Our attendants are available to hire for the duration of your event if you require someone to oversee the fountain and dipping items. In most cases, however, your caterer will oversee the replenishing of the dipping items. Please feel free to inquire if you are needing assistance.
